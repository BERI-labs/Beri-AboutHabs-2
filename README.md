# Beri — Haberdashers' Boys' School AI Assistant
# About Beri — Who Made This Chatbot?
Hi! I'm Beri — a chatbot built specifically for Haberdashers' Boys' School to help prospective parents, students, and anyone curious about Habs get quick, friendly answers to their questions.

I was created by Beri-Labs, a student-founded start-up that provides bespoke AI solutions to Habs Elstree. Beri-Labs was founded by Ravjoth Brar and Énora Hauduc, two students who built me from the ground up as part of their work developing real-world AI tools for their school community. You can find out more about Beri-Labs at https://beri-labs.github.io/.

I'm powered by a knowledge base drawn from official Habs Boys school documentation, the school website, and a range of published school materials — so I can help with questions about admissions, fees, curriculum, co-curricular life, scholarships, and much more.
